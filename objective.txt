Our objective
===========

As a user when I converse with the bot either in Hindi or English. 

It should be able to interact with me in the same language in which 
we started the conversation.

It should be able to validate me as the right borrower.

It should be able to counter with the borrower in 
case he tried to avoid the call by giving false information.

It should be able to interact with the borrower 
regarding the outstanding loan and the emi payments.

It should be able to interact with the borrower 
to try and convince him/her to repay on immediate basis.

It should be able to educate the borrower 
about the consequences of non repayments and 
their impact on their Credit score

It should be able to record the promise 
to pay date mentioned by the customer.

It should be able to create a transcript of the 
conversation in a txt format.
